Who’s using Rest Assured?

Residential Provider Agencies

Rest Assured is being used in waiver settings, intermediate care facilities (ICF), and by home health agencies to maximize staffing efficiencies by providing a safe alternative to hands-on care, when appropriate, and/or add an extra level of safety.

Family Caregivers & Guardians

Caregivers use Rest Assured to help aging parents and disabled loved ones live as independently as possible.
