OatUP is a dairy free milk alternative from the good people at Dirty Clean Food (DCF), helping you squeeze every last drop of goodness out of your day. DCF is a young company with big plans, driven by the belief that healthy food comes from nurturing the land and farming as nature intended.

As the only plant-based milk in the world that is carbon-neutral certified and made using Western Australian (WA) oats, when you buy OatUP you are choosing to load up on nutrients, show up for your community and step up for the planet. Right at home on your granola, in your smoothie, your baking or your latte, OatUP slots quietly and conveniently into your fridge and your lifestyle.
